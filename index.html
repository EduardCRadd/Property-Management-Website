Lifestyle & Services

I. Block Management Services

We manage residential properties in London with a professional service. From preparing and administering service charge demands to undertaking monthly check-ins with staff to attending the AGM, our approach is considered and committed to high quality. We build a long-term strategy to ensure an efficient and transparent maintenance regime tailored to the individual property requirements.

II.Insurance Services

We are members of the RICS Designated Professional Body Scheme. We are regulated for general insurance distribution activities in the UK, on behalf of the Financial Conduct Authority, under a programme approved by HM Treasury. We can therefore provide insurance advice and quotations for the majority of relevant insurance covers. This is arranged in conjunction with our retained insurance brokers: Chambers and Newman.

III.Accounting Services

Service charge accounting for properties is all part of our service. Accounts are drafted by our expert in-house accountancy team, ready for independent certification. The supporting balance sheet is prepared in accordance with the relevant framework. All monies collected for each property can be tracked and reconciled.

IV.Project Management Services

We can oversee projects from start to finish, whether for undertaking internal or external repairs and redecorations or replacing mechanical and electrical equipment such as lifts. We either use our skilled in-house team or contract out to trusted consultants if specialist expertise is required.
